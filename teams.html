Meeting Privacy and Security - Microsoft Teams

Meeting Recording and Access

  • The meeting **organizer** has the ability to record the meeting.
  • Participants will be notified if a meeting is being recorded.
  • Recorded meetings may be stored and accessed by authorized personnel.
  • If you do not consent to being recorded, please inform the organizer before the meeting starts.

Privacy and Confidentiality

  • Meeting discussions and shared content are confidential and should not be disclosed outside of authorized attendees.
  • Participants should not record or share meeting content without explicit permission.
  • Only authorized individuals should join the meeting.

Security and Best Practices

  • Do not share the meeting link publicly or with unauthorized persons.
  • Ensure your Microsoft Teams application is updated to the latest version for security enhancements.
  • Use a secure internet connection to prevent disruptions.
  • Report any suspicious activity to the meeting organizer immediately.

Additional Information

For more details regarding Microsoft Teams privacy and security policies, please refer to the official Microsoft Teams Security & Compliance Overview.
